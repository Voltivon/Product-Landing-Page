Main Characters

Master Chief Petty Officer John-117,[Note 1] more commonly known as Master Chief, is a SPARTAN-II commando of the UNSC Naval Special Warfare Command. He is the protagonist and main character in both the Halo trilogy and Reclaimer Saga.

Thel 'Vadam (formerly Thel 'Vadamee),[5] often simply referred to as the Arbiter, is the deuteragonist of both Halo 2 and Halo 3, and is a supporting character in Halo 5: Guardians. He is the most prominent Sangheili shown throughout those games.

Sergeant Major Avery Junior Johnson (SN: 48789-20114-AJ)[5] was a Human senior non-commissioned officer who served with the UNSC Marine Corps during the Insurrection and Human-Covenant war

Cortana, UNSC Artificial intelligence (SN: CTN 0452-9), was a smart artificial intelligence construct. She was one of the most important figures in the Human-Covenant war, and was also John-117's partner in various combat missions as well as serving as the AI
